About Me

Data at scale

I'm a database engineer at Microsoft — and I've spent over a decade making data systems that don't break.

Specializing in SQL Server administration and development, I bring deep experience managing large-scale, mission-critical data environments where downtime isn't an option and performance is everything.

My expertise spans database replication, mirroring, log shipping, query performance optimization, and cloud data warehousing. I've architected replication pipelines across 20+ global servers, designed high-availability solutions, and optimized queries over billions of rows — all while keeping systems running 24/7.

Before Microsoft, I spent nearly 9 years at iHerb as a Senior DBA, where I built and managed the data backbone of a global e-commerce platform. I then worked at Universal Electronics as a Senior Data Engineer, designing pipelines for consumer electronics operations worldwide.

Beyond databases, I've been actively expanding into AI — earning certifications in Azure AI, Generative AI, and Agentic AI architectures. I believe the future of data engineering is deeply intertwined with intelligent systems.

I'm based in Seattle, WA and open to conversations about engineering, data architecture, and cloud strategy.
